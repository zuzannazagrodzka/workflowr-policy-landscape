1b. Dictionaries preparation

We created three dictionaries: UNESCO dictionary, business dictionary and book dictionary.

UNESCO (Open Research) dictionary
The first dictionary (UNESCO (Open Research) dictionary) was created by removing stop words and choosing 100 most frequent words in the UNESCO Recommendations on Open Science (https://unesdoc.unesco.org/ark:/48223/pf0000379949.locale=en).

Business dictionary
The second one (Business dictionary) was created by importing business glossaries from four websites (see below). 100 words that occurred the most frequent were used to create this dictionary. Websites: https://www.theguardian.com/business/glossary-business-terms-a-z-jargon https://www.investopedia.com/ https://www.economist.com/economics-a-to-z https://www.workspace.co.uk/content-hub/business-insight/glossary-of-business-terminology

Control dictionary
The third one (Control dictionary) was created by removing stop words and identifying 100 most common words in the ecology book Our Vanishing Wildlife downloaded from the online library Project Gutenberg. https://www.goodreads.com/book/show/1302950.Our_Vanishing_Wildlife
